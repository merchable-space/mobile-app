Release Notes - v{{currentAppVersion}}

 What's New
  • The Dashboard has had a visual upgrade, and the background for the app is a little more colourful.
  • You can now bulk update product variations, setting their stock levels and prices all at once.
  • The All Products page has had a renovation, including smaller product images to increase screen estate usage.
  • If a product or any of its variations are low on or out of stock, the main product title on the All Products will now be coloured to reflect that status.
  • The Update Product section has also had a visual upgrade, making it easier to use and take up less space on the screen.
